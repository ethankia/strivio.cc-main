About Strivio

A small studio with one focus: results, not just looks.

Strivio is a web design and development studio based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. We build websites for businesses that want more than something that just sits online.

Our approach

A website is a tool, not a trophy.

Too many small business websites get built once, look fine on launch day, and then quietly stop working — slow, outdated, or hard to find anything on. We started Strivio to fix that pattern: build it right, keep it fast, and make sure every page has a clear job to do.

That means we spend as much time on structure and speed as we do on how a page looks. A beautiful site that loads slowly or confuses visitors isn't doing its job — and its job is to bring you customers.

What guides how we work

  • Built for your business, not a template. Every site starts from your content and your customers, not a stock layout.
  • Speed is a feature. A fast site keeps people around long enough to become customers.
  • You talk to the person doing the work. No account managers passing your project down the line.
  • Local and reachable. Based in Grand Rapids — a call or a coffee away, not a support ticket.
